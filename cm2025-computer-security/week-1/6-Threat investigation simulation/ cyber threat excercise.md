Imagine that you are a cyber threat analyst. Your task is to examine a fictional threat report and assess the credibility, impact and relevance of the intelligence provided. Based on your analysis, suggest appropriate mitigation strategies that align with best practices.

## Instructions

Download the Cyber Threat Report (see below).
Open the report. This report includes technical indicators, attack behaviour and contextual information related to a recent phishing and ransomware campaign targeting a university.

Draft a written response (approximately 500–800 words) that includes the following details. 

An analysis of the threat report

In your analysis, include:

the types of intelligence present (e.g. technical indicators, attack patterns, attacker motivations)

the credibility and relevance of the intelligence sources

potential vulnerabilities and weaknesses within the organisation being targeted.

## Your recommendations

List at least three mitigation strategies you would recommend implementing immediately.

For each, indicate:

which layer of defence it addresses (e.g. access controls, endpoint security, monitoring)

how it mitigates the specific threat described

any challenges the organisation might face in implementing it.

## Optional extension activity

How would your recommendations change if you were advising a different type of organisation (e.g. healthcare or finance)?

What intelligence would you prioritise if time and resources were limited?
