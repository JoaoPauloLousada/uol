# Bella Italia Chatbot - Core Intents

## 1. Greeting
**Purpose**: Welcome customers and start the conversation
**Examples**:
- "Hello"
- "Hi"
- "Good morning"
- "Hey"

## 2. Menu Inquiry
**Purpose**: Show the menu or specific categories
**Examples**:
- "Show me the menu"
- "What do you have?"
- "What's on the menu?"
- "List the items"
- "What pastas do you have?"

## 3. Item Details
**Purpose**: Get information about specific dishes
**Examples**:
- "Tell me about Spaghetti Carbonara"
- "What is Lasagna alla Bolognese?"
- "Describe the Caesar Salad"
- "What's in the Ravioli?"

## 4. Place Order
**Purpose**: Add items to the cart/order
**Examples**:
- "I want 2 Spaghetti Carbonara"
- "Add a Caesar Salad"
- "Order Penne Arrabbiata"
- "I'll have one Lasagna"
- "Give me 2 red wines"

## 5. View Cart
**Purpose**: Show current order and total
**Examples**:
- "Show my order"
- "What did I order?"
- "View cart"
- "What's in my cart?"
- "Checkout"

## 6. Price Inquiry
**Purpose**: Check prices of specific items
**Examples**:
- "How much is Spaghetti Carbonara?"
- "Price of Caesar Salad"
- "What does the Lasagna cost?"
- "How much for red wine?"

## 7. Restaurant Info
**Purpose**: Provide restaurant information (hours, location)
**Examples**:
- "What are your hours?"
- "When do you open?"
- "Where are you located?"
- "What's your address?"
- "Location please"

## 8. Goodbye
**Purpose**: End the conversation
**Examples**:
- "Bye"
- "Goodbye"
- "Thank you"
- "Exit"
- "Quit"
